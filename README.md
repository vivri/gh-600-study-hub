# GH-600 Study Hub

An interactive, single-file study webpage for **Exam GH-600 — Developing in Agentic AI Systems** (GitHub Certified: Agentic AI Developer).

**Usage:** open `index.html` in a browser (internet needed only for the Bootstrap CDN).

## What's inside

- **Exam facts** — pass score, format, beta/GA timeline
- **Skills Measured** — all 6 domains as a checkable study tracker (progress saved in localStorage)
- **Deep Study Notes** — distilled notes from all 15 content units of the three Microsoft Learn training modules
- **Flashcards** — 67 interactive flip cards, filterable by domain
- **Practice** — a 15-question instant-feedback quiz and a 41-question graded mock exam with timer and per-domain scoring
- **Labs & 4-week plan** — hands-on lab list and study sequence
- **Study Resources** — succinct summaries of every resource in the official study guide
